KEY COMPONENTS OF WASTE MANAGEMENTS



  • Waste Audit: Conducting a waste audit helps businesses identify the type, quantity, and frequency of waste produced, enabling them to create effective waste management plans and track progress
  • Waste Disposal Methods: Different methods are used depending on the type and amount of waste, such as recycling, composting, incineration, and waste-to-energy conversion.


  • Circular Economy: In a circular economy, waste is managed by sorting and preparing it for reuse or recycling, reducing the need for raw materials and minimizing environmental impact
