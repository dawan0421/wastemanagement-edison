THE IMPORTANCE OF WASTE MANAGEMENT

THIS WEBSITE IS ALL ABOUT THE IMPORTANCE OF WASTE MANAGEMENT



here are some reasons:


Environmental Protection: Proper waste management helps prevent environmental damage and pollution, ensuring a healthier environment for future generations. Health and Safety: Effective waste management reduces the risk of health issues associated with waste handling and disposal. Economic Efficiency: Waste management is essential for building sustainable and liveable cities, and its proper implementation can reduce costs and enhance economic efficiency. Community Engagement: Community involvement in waste management practices is crucial for maintaining a robust waste management system and promoting sustainable practices
